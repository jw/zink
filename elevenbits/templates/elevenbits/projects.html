Klink

Klink is a wedding application. It allows the bride and groom to inform their friends and family to invite them to the wedding. Visitors can log in, to see some pictures and read some interesting info on the wedding. Also a route plan and gifts are part of the web site.

The website is based on PHP and will work with a MySQL database. A nice feature is the cataloging of visitors of the wedding party. It is handy to allow some people for the full day(s), others only for the mass, yet others for the reception.
